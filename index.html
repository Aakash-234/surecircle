About Sure Circle

Reimagining insurance through trusted communities and transparent technology

Our Story

Sure Circle was born from a simple frustration: paying ₹18,000 in smartphone insurance premiums over 18 months without ever filing a claim. That money was gone forever, lost to overheads and administrative costs, with no return for the policyholder.

We asked ourselves: What if there was a better way? What if people could pool resources with those they trust, only paying when someone genuinely needs help, and getting their money back when no claims are made?

That's how Sure Circle came to life - as India's first tech-enabled peer-to-peer self-insurance platform, built for the digital generation that values transparency, community, and smart financial decisions.

How We're Different

Community-Driven

Insurance pools formed by people who trust each other - friends, family, colleagues, or communities with shared interests.

Fully Transparent

Every contribution, claim, and vote is visible to pool members. No hidden fees, no complex terms, no surprise rejections.

Money Back

Unlike traditional insurance, unused funds are returned to members. You're not just paying premiums - you're building community wealth.

Democratic Decisions

Claims are decided by the community through transparent voting, with multiple governance models to choose from.

Our Technology Moats

Behavioral & Trust Graph

We're building India's largest risk-behavior database outside traditional insurers. Our proprietary SureScore system analyzes claims history, voting patterns, and community engagement to create trust profiles that power micro-insurance across platforms.

Sticky Communities

Self-organized groups create strong social bonds that are hard to replicate. With in-group features, voting leaderboards, and longevity benefits, we're building the social glue of modern digital insurance.

Float Optimization Engine

Our automated, RBI-compliant float management system optimizes returns on pooled funds through smart allocation in FDs, liquid funds, and approved instruments, creating the best risk-adjusted treasury layer.

Regulatory Compliance

Sure Circle operates within India's regulatory framework as a technology platform facilitating peer-to-peer financial pooling. We work closely with legal experts to ensure compliance with:

  • Reserve Bank of India (RBI) guidelines for financial services
  • Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India (IRDAI) regulations
  • Data protection and privacy laws
  • Financial transaction and reporting requirements

Important: Sure Circle facilitates community-based risk pooling and is not a licensed insurance company. Payouts are subject to community decisions and pool availability.

Our Mission

"To democratize insurance in India by empowering communities to protect each other through transparency, technology, and trust."

We're not just building an insurance alternative - we're creating a movement towards community-driven financial protection that puts people first, eliminates unnecessary overhead, and rewards responsible behavior.
